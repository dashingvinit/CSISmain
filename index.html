About CSIS 2023

We are thrilled to announce that this year, Chandigarh University has the honor and privilege to host the prestigious Computer Society India Symposium (CSIS) 2023. CSIS is a remarkable convergence of technology enthusiasts, industry experts, and creative minds, all driven by a shared passion for the transformative power of technology. Join us as we delve into a series of captivating events designed to inspire, educate, and ignite innovation.

At CSIS 2023, the stage is set for an array of captivating events that will ignite your imagination, expand your knowledge, and leave an indelible mark on your tech-driven soul. Here's a glimpse of what awaits you:

Paper Presentation

Witness groundbreaking research and innovation as young visionaries present their latest findings and ideas

Panel Discussion

Engage in thought-provoking conversations with industry leaders, researchers, and influencers, delving into the future impact of technology.

Digital Art Exhibition

Immerse yourself in a realm where creativity meets technology, as digital art transcends boundaries and captivates the senses.

Project Exhibition

Explore a showcase of cutting-edge projects developed by brilliant minds, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the tech world.

Cultural Event

Celebrate diversity and camaraderie as we come together to honor our collective passions beyond technology, through music, dance, and tradition.

Hands-On Workshop

: Dive deep into the heart of technology through immersive workshops, where practical skills and knowledge are imparted by seasoned professionals.

CSIS 2023 promises to be an unforgettable experience, where you will connect with like-minded individuals, discover the forefront of technology, and be inspired to shape the future. Get ready to unleash the power of technology and be a part of this extraordinary journey at Chandigarh University
